White Sauce Pasta

Home

Description

White sauce pasta is a creamy and delicious dish made with al dente pasta, a rich white sauce, and a blend of cheeses. It's often flavored with herbs and spices, making it a comforting and satisfying meal perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pasta

    • Fettuccine or Penne Pasta

  • White Sauce

    • Butter
    • Flour
    • Milk
    • Parmesan Cheese
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper

  • Toppings

    • Chopped Herbs (e.g., Parsley)
    • Garlic
    • Grated Mozzarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta

    1. Boil Water
    2. Add Pasta and Cook Al Dente
    3. Drain and Set Aside

  2. Prepare White Sauce

    1. Melt Butter
    2. Add Flour to Make Roux
    3. Gradually Add Milk
    4. Stir in Parmesan Cheese

  3. Combine Pasta and Sauce

    1. Mix Cooked Pasta with White Sauce
    2. Add Chopped Herbs and Garlic

  4. Top and Serve

    1. Sprinkle Mozzarella Cheese
    2. Serve Hot
